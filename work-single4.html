Overview

Super Sparty Brothers is a 2D side scrolling platform game. Your goal as Sparty is to run and jump around the world, collecting coins (points,) shields (extra lives,) and ultimately the rose (for the win!) while avoiding enemies and harmful terrain. For my goal in this project, I expanded upon the base game that we created in class and designed one fully-functional and completed level.

This game demo of Super Sparty Brothers was created for Michigan State University’s Coursera course on Game Development for Modern Platforms. A large portion of the game assets and the game development was designed by the professor and was recreated by me in the class.

There are some additional assets of my own design and development that have been added to this game; additionally, the level design is 100% my own creation.
